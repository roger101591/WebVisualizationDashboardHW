Latitude Analysis

This analysis explain the relationships between Latitude vs. Temperature, Latitude vs. Cloudiness, Latitude vs. Humidity, and Latitude vs. Wind Speed. 500 cities were randomly chosen as the sample set for this analysis, taking their latitudes and weather data on 3/16/2018-the date of analysis.

We used Matplotlib and OpenWeatherMap API to collect and process weather data from these 500 cities. We expect a positive correlation between Latitude and Temperature. However, we do not expect any relationship between Latitude and Wind Speed or Cloudiness or Humidity.
